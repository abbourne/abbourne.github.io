About Bill Bourne

July 02, 2013
I started programming in 1972 (Fortran, APL, assembler) Even though my university degree was in Electrical Engineering, my whole professional career has been software related. I became a manager in 1981, although I continued to write production code until 1986. I’ve tried to keep my hand in, even as I became a senior manager and a director. I’ve been a principal founder of a video games start-up, had 2 games on the Billboard top 40, and wrote applications for Apple and Microsoft.
